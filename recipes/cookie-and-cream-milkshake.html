Cookie & Cream Milkshake

Description

Chick Fil A Cookies & Cream Milkshake is exactly like the one we all love, except you can make it at home.

Now if you have a craving a Chick Fil A Milkshake on a Sunday, you don’t have to go without!

You only need 4 ingredients and you probably have them on hand already: Oreo, Ice Cream, Milk and Vanilla Extract.

You can also add whip cream and a cherry on top, but it’s optional, not necessary!

Ingredient

  • 6 oreo cookies
  • 2 1/2 cups french vanilla ice cream, softened
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • whipped cream, optional
  • two maraschino cherries, optional

Steps

  1. In a blender, crush cookies with a few good pulses.
  2. Add ice cream, milk and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into 2 tall glasses and garnish each with whipped cream and a maraschino cherry.
  4. And enjoy!
